## Router: setSchedule Mutation

- Input: `{ id: uuid, scheduledAt: coerce.date }`
- Reads `before` with `findUniqueOrThrow`, updates `{ scheduledAt, timeConfirmed: true }`
- Catches Prisma `P2002` → `TRPCError({ code: 'CONFLICT' })`
- Logs `type: 'status_changed'`, body `Chốt giờ họp PH: <ISO>`, `changes:[{field:'scheduledAt', old, new}]`
- Wrapped in `withRls(rlsContextOf(ctx.session), …)` matching `setStatus` pattern

## Parent Portal Rendering

When `m.timeConfirmed === false`: Badge shows `toLocaleDateString('vi-VN')` (date only) + muted `(chưa chốt giờ)` Text beside it.
When `m.timeConfirmed === true`: Badge shows `fmtDateTime(m.scheduledAt)` (unchanged behaviour).
